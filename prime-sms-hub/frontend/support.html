Numbers are active for 20 minutes by default. You can extend the duration if needed through your account settings.
How do I fund my wallet?
Visit the "Fund Wallet" section and choose between Manual Deposit (bank transfer) or VPay (automatic). Follow the instructions for your chosen method.

We accept bank transfers (manual deposits) and VPay automatic payments. Both methods are fast and secure.

How can I track my orders?
