Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly are SMS messages delivered?

Our platform typically delivers messages within 3-5 seconds. However, delivery times may vary depending on the recipient's network and location.

What file formats can I upload for payment verification?

We accept JPG, PNG, and PDF files for payment verification. Maximum file size is 5MB.

How long does payment verification take?

Verification usually takes 1-2 hours during business hours (9AM-6PM GMT). Outside these hours, it may take up to 12 hours.

Can I send international SMS messages?

Yes, our platform supports sending messages to over 200 countries worldwide. Pricing varies by country.

Is there an API available for developers?

Yes, we provide a comprehensive REST API with documentation and sample code for easy integration with your applications.

What happens if my SMS fails to deliver?

Failed deliveries are automatically retried up to 3 times. If delivery still fails, the credit is returned to your account.
