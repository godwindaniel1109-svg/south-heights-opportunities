Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs in a bowl with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat butter or oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour the eggs into the pan and tilt to spread evenly.
  4. When the eggs begin to set, add your fillings on one side.
  5. Fold the omelette in half and cook for another minute.
  6. Slide onto a plate and serve hot.
