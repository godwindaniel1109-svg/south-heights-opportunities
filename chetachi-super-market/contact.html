Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

You can track your order by clicking on "Track Order" in the quick actions section or by using your order number and email address on our tracking page.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be in their original condition with all tags and packaging. Please visit our return policy page for detailed information.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we ship within Nigeria. We are working on expanding our shipping services to other African countries and internationally.

How can I become a member?

You can become a member by creating an account on our website. Membership is free and comes with benefits like exclusive deals, reward points, and early access to sales.
